Are you ready for a zombie apocalypse?

Overview!

In this interactive story or game, you and your group will try to survive a zombie apocalypse taken place in your city. You guys trapped in your highschool classroom and your goal is to exit the school without losing your life. You will be given choices and out of the choices, you must choose the ones that will lead you out. You will be facing a series of challenges and losses, don't let it slow you down! Make sure you and your group makes it out alive. The characters are you, Alissa, John, Joy, and Robert.

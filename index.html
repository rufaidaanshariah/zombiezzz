Beware...

Overview!

In this interactive story or game, you or your group will try to survive a zombie apocalypse taken place in your city. You guys trapped in your highschool classroom and your goal is to exit the school without losing your life. You will be given choices and out of the choices, you must choose the ones that will lead you out. You will be facing a series of challenges and losses, don't let it slow you down! Make sure you and your group makes it out alive. The characters are you, Alissa, John, Joy, and Robert.

Rules!

1. Once you've made your choice, you cannot go back and change it. All choices are final.

2. Three out of the five people in your group must survive in order to survive or finish the game.

3.Make sure your health bar doesn't deplete, or else it'll lead to your demise D:
